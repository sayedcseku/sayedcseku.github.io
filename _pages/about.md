Welcome to my Homepage. I am Md. Abu Sayed, Lecturer of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Millennium University, Bangladesh. I have completed my Bachelor from Khulna University, Bangladesh with a major in Computer Science and Engineering in January 2019. My undergraduate thesis was supervised by Professor Dr. G M Atiqur Rahaman (Computational Color and Spectral Image Analysis Lab, Khulna University) and co-supervised by Dr. Sajib Saha (Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation, Australia)

Undergraduate courses such as Artificial Intelligence, Image Processing, Pattern Recognition, and Data Mining, etc. made me interested in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Data Science. Which helped me in my thesis titled "Automated method to segment retinal blood vessels from color fundus photographs".

I am interested in exploring and enriching my knowledge in "Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Deep Learning in Health-care, Sports or Software Engineering".
